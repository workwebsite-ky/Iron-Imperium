Common Questions

FAQ

Do you offer a free trial?
Yes! All new members receive a complimentary 7-day trial pass with full access to the facility, group classes, and a free fitness assessment session.
Is Iron Imperium suitable for beginners?
Absolutely. Our coaches specialize in onboarding new members — from your first session, you'll receive a full orientation and a customized beginner program.
What are your operating hours?
Iron Imperium is open 24 hours a day, 7 days a week, 365 days a year. Staffed hours are 5AM–11PM. Key fob access is available for off-hours.
Can I freeze or cancel my membership?
Yes — members can freeze their membership for up to 3 months per year for any reason. Cancellation requires 30 days written notice after any minimum commitment period.
Do you offer nutrition coaching?
Yes. Our Imperium Elite and Platinum packages include dedicated nutrition coaching and meal planning. Standalone nutrition sessions can also be booked separately.

Pricing FAQs
Are contracts required?
No long-term contracts are required for month-to-month plans. Annual plans with discounts are also available.
Is there a joining fee?
There is a one-time $25 enrollment fee for new members. This is waived during our promotional periods.
Can I upgrade my plan later?
Yes! You can upgrade your membership at any time and we'll prorate the difference from your current billing cycle.

The Iron Story

Iron Imperium was born from a single obsession: to create the gym that we always wished existed. Not a place that just had machines and mirrors, but a true institution — a place of discipline, excellence, and transformation.

Founded over a decade ago by a team of competitive athletes and fitness professionals, we built Iron Imperium from the ground up with one clear philosophy: the environment you train in shapes the athlete you become.

Today, Iron Imperium stands as the city's premier fitness destination — home to thousands of members ranging from complete beginners to professional athletes, all united by the pursuit of something greater than their current self.
